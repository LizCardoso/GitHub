10 Upper Bank Street
10 Upper Bank Street is a 32-story office building located in Canary Wharf, in the Docklands area of London. It was completed in 2003 and is 151 m (495 ft) tall.It was designed by the architects Kohn Pedersen Fox and built by Canary Wharf Contractors.[1]Most of the building is occupied by the law firm Clifford Chance, and serves as its world headquarters.
